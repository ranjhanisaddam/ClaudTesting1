How We Work

Our Engagement Methodology

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all frameworks. Every engagement begins with deep listening and ends with measurable impact. This is the Meridian way.

01
Rapid Situational Assessment
Within the first 72 hours, our team conducts an intensive diagnostic — financial, operational, and organizational. We identify the true problem, not just its symptoms, and align on a shared understanding of the challenge.
02
Hypothesis-Driven Analysis
We move fast by being structured. Our teams formulate and pressure-test hypotheses with data, forming a clear point-of-view on the key levers of value creation before investing in exhaustive analysis.
03
Actionable Recommendations
We deliver a prioritized, sequenced roadmap — with owners, timelines, and measurable milestones. Every recommendation is stress-tested for implementation feasibility, not just analytical elegance.
04
Embedded Execution Support
Optionally, we stay and do. Our advisors can serve in operating roles, run programme management offices, or coach internal teams through transformation — ensuring momentum never stalls.
