Overview

High-impact outcomes through strategic advice and practical delivery.

Meridian & Co established a permanent presence in Dubai in 2008 and expanded to Abu Dhabi in 2017, serving the Middle East and North Africa region with world-class advisory services across finance, operations, and investigations.

Our team of experienced professionals brings a 360-degree approach to problem-solving, driving the critical change required to optimize performance and maximize value for organizations across the region.

Serving as trusted partners to executive boards and in interim management roles, our bias toward action and roll-up-your-sleeves culture ensures we deliver tangible results — rapidly and sustainably.
